About Me

My name is Cayden Scruggs. I am a freshman at the University of South Carolina. I have a lot of interest in computers and programming, though I am also very interested in politics, psychology, and nature. I began this year as a Political Science major, then Psychology, then Cyber-Intelligence, and am now trying to get a Major Information Meeting in order to change to Computer Science. In regards to programming, I got started with trying to make Minecraft modpacks. I would go in and edit .json files and configs and enjoyed the syntax and translation of text to action so much that I began to research programming, and especially hacking, and ended up downloading a game called Bitburner. This game uses Javascript to simuate hacking in a Cyberpunk world, and it really sparked some passion for programming and cybersecurity in me. I went on to do as much research into these fields as I could, and made some much needed updates to my security such as using password managers, mobile authenticators, and making the move to Linux. Outside of programming, I really enjoy psychology with specific interest towards abnormal psychology and mental illness. The ways the human brain can function, and disfunction, are incredible to me. Recently, I have been reminded of a younger love of nature and have designed and built my first Paludarium (essentially a terrarium with a water feature). This has been one of my most enjoyable projects to date, and I love the ways its connected me to the outside world. I originally built it with the hopes of housing a garter snake (I love snakes) but it is unfortunately not quite up to snuff for this goal. Now, the tank houses two ghost shrimp, some bladder snails, many Armadillidium vulgare isopods (roly polies), some native millipedes and some temperate springtails. The tank is meant to include only native South Carolina plants and animals (I am unsure about the springtails but they are a vital component of the clean up crew!). I also love skateboarding, and oftentimes you can find me skating outside of Russell House.
